Refund Policy

1. Perishable Goods Policy

Because our cakes, cookies, and cupcakes are perishable food items made fresh to order, we generally do not accept returns. Unlike clothing or electronics, we cannot resell food items once they have left our shop.

  • We do not offer refunds for "change of mind" or taste preference.
  • Please review your invoice carefully before payment to ensure flavor, size, and design details are correct.

2. Damages & Issues

Please inspect your order immediately upon reception (pickup or delivery).

  • Delivery Damages: If you used a third-party courier booked by us, and the item arrives smashed or ruined, please take a photo immediately and contact us within 1 hour of receipt.
  • Client Handling: We are not responsible for damages caused by improper handling or storage after the item has been handed over to you or your courier.
  • Missing Items: If an item is missing from your box, let us know immediately so we can arrange a refund for the missing portion or send a replacement.

3. Cancellation Policy

Since we bake our goods fresh, cancellations affect our production schedule and ingredients.

  • 7 Days Prior: Full refund available.
  • 3-6 Days Prior: 50% refund (to cover reserved ingredients/slots).
  • 48 Hours or Less: Non-refundable. The batter is likely already prepped!

4. Custom Design Accuracy

For custom cakes and cupcakes, we do our best to match your provided reference photos. However, please note that baking is an art form and icing colors may vary slightly. Slight variations in design interpretation are not grounds for a refund.
