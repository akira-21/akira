Terms & Conditions

1. Agreement to Terms

By placing an order with DreamDoughPH (via website, social media, or phone), you agree to be bound by these Terms and Conditions. Please read them carefully before finalizing your purchase.

2. Orders and Payment

  • Booking Confirmation: Orders are only confirmed once full payment or the required deposit has been received. Unpaid slots will be released after 24 hours.
  • Lead Time: Custom cakes require at least 5-7 days notice. Cookies and standard cupcakes require at least 2-3 days notice.
  • Payment Methods: We accept GCash, Bank Transfer, or Cash (for in-store pickup only).

3. Pickup and Delivery

Transfer of Responsibility: Once the product leaves our premises, DreamDoughPH is no longer liable for any damage.

  • Self-Pickup: Please handle cakes with care. Place them on the floor of the car (flattest surface), not on the seats. Keep air conditioning on high.
  • Third-Party Couriers (Grab/Lalamove): We recommend using "Car" options for cakes. Motorbikes are strictly discouraged for tiered cakes. We are not liable for damages caused by the rider's handling or traffic conditions.

4. Storage & Handling

Our products are made with real butter and dairy. They are sensitive to heat, especially in the Philippines climate.

  • Cakes/Cupcakes: Keep refrigerated. Bring to room temperature 30 minutes before serving for the best texture.
  • Cookies: Store in an airtight container at room temperature. Consume within 5 days for freshness.

5. Design Variations

Baking is an art form. While we aim to replicate your requested design or reference photos as closely as possible, please allow for slight variations in color, piping style, and placement. We reserve the right to make artistic adjustments to ensure structural stability.

6. Allergies & Dietary Restrictions

Our kitchen processes flour, eggs, dairy, nuts, and chocolate. While we take hygiene seriously, we cannot guarantee a 100% allergen-free environment due to the possibility of cross-contamination.
