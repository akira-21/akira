Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

You can order directly through our website by adding items to your cart and proceeding to checkout! Alternatively, you can message us on our Facebook or Instagram page for custom cake inquiries. Please note that all orders are subject to slot availability.

How far in advance should I order?

Custom Cakes: We recommend booking at least 5-7 days in advance to ensure we have enough time to perfect your design.

Cookies & Cupcakes: For standard orders, 2-3 days notice is usually sufficient. For bulk orders (50+ pcs), please give us at least 1 week's notice.

Do you deliver?

We do not have an in-house delivery team, but we can help book a third-party courier (Grab, Lalamove, Borzo) for you. The delivery fee will be shouldered by the buyer.

Tip: For tiered cakes, we strictly require a "Car" option (MPV/Sedan) to ensure the cake arrives safely. We are not liable for motorbike mishaps!

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept GCash, Bank Transfer (BDO/BPI), and Cash (only for store pickups). Full payment is required to confirm your slot for custom orders.

Do you cater to allergies (Gluten-free, Nut-free)?

While we take hygiene very seriously, our kitchen processes flour, eggs, dairy, nuts, and chocolate daily. We cannot guarantee a 100% allergen-free environment due to the risk of cross-contamination.

How should I store my cake?

Cakes with Buttercream: Keep refrigerated. Take it out 30-45 minutes before serving to let the butter soften for the best taste!

Cookies: Store in an airtight container at room temperature. They are best consumed within 5 days.

Can I customize the flavor and design?

Absolutely! That is our specialty. You can choose from our flavor list (Chocolate, Vanilla, Red Velvet, Mocha) and send us your design peg. Prices for custom designs vary based on complexity.
